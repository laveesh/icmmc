KEYNOTE SPEAKER

Prof. Peeyush Chandra

Retd. Professor (of mathematics) from I.I.T. Kanpur

Retd. Professor (of mathematics) from I.I.T. Kanpur. His Research areas are: Mathematical Biology, Mathematical Modeling,Fluiddynamics. He has about 100 research papers and guided 13 Ph.Ds.
He was President of Indian Mathematical Society, and also of IndianSociety of theoretical and Applied Mathematics. Presently Academic Secretary of Indian Mathematical Society

Prof. Purshotam Chaturani

Retd. Professor (of mathematics) from I.I.T. Bombay

Retd. Professor (of mathematics) from I.I.T. Bombay. He has Doctoral and Post-Doctoral Research Experience: 50 yrs. His Research ares are Biomechanics (Blood Flow), Shock Waves, Non-Newtonian Fluid Mechanics. He has about 100 research papers in international and national journals.He has also delivered lectures at Windsor Univ., Winnepeg Univ., Western Ontario, London(Canada), Yale Univ.(USA).

PROF. K.S.Nisar

Prince Sattam bin Abdul-Aziz University,Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Associate Professor, Department of Mathematics, Prince Sattam bin Abdul-Aziz University,Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. His Research areas are: Special Functions and Trigonometric series/sums., Hypergeometric Series, Calculus, Approximation Theory, SAC-OCDMA code networks, Machine learning, System Identifications and Mathematical. He has about 100 research papers and 3 books. Won best researcher award and outstanding publication award at PSAA University. He is editorial board member many journal including Springer Plus journal, American Mathematical Society, MathScinet andSCI bay journal of Mathematics etc.
